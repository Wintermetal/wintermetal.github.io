Ability Information

  • Abilities: She is basically a ship in human form. She can traverse on water quite quickly and she has a heavy broadside of 36 cannons.
    Compared to a normal human, she can take a lot more hits (damage?) as she has the same durability as a warship, but she weighs about 10 881 times more than a normal human of her size. She needs special equipment to use her power to their full extent.

  • Strengths: Very tough skin, She can traverse along water relatively fast. She can go for long periods of time without eating or drinking anything. As a side effect of her weight she is quite strong (She can momentarily lift up to ~1000 tonnes).

  • Weaknesses: She weighs a lot. She uses a lot more energy compared to a normal person and has to eat accordingly. And everything that comes with a weight of 935.8 tonnes.

  • Counters against the ability: land, off-shore, ground,
  • Ability moves: Broadside! (Fires all cannons from one side),

Backstory

She served in the Royal Navy from 1625 to 1668. She fought in numerous battles including her maiden voyage in Càdiz. After retiring from the Royal Navy, she sailed the seas wondering what to do with her time and knowledge and decided she would pass down her knowledge to a new generation who might need it.
