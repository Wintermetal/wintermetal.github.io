𝒜𝒷𝒾𝓁𝒾𝓉𝓎 𝐼𝓃𝒻𝑜𝓇𝓂𝒶𝓉𝒾𝑜𝓃

  • Abilities: Vesihiisi is a shipgirl (a submarine) so she has the same abilities as a normal (real life) submarine. This includes firing torpedoes (6), AA-guns (3) and laying seamines (20). She cannot dive deeper than 75 m (246 ft)
  • Strengths: Very tough skin, She can traverse along water relatively fast (23,3 km/h on surface, 15.7 km/h submerged) and can stay underwater for 20 hours before she has to resurface. Can go for 150 hours without eating or drinking anything. As a side effect of her weight she is quite strong (She can momentarily lift up to ~500 tonnes of weight).
  • Weaknesses: She weighs a lot. She uses a lot more energy compared to a normal person and has to eat accordingly. After not eating or drinking for 150 hours she has to eat ~30 tonnes of food and drink 20 tonnes of diesel. After diving she has a state of higher energy consumption as she "recharges". Recharge time is dependant on the time she was underwater. She emits a quiet rumbling noise (like a quiet diesel engine) at all times except when she sleeps. The more she stresses herself physcally, the louder and faster the noise.
  • Drawbacks: Her abilities work only in water and with her rigging.

𝓑𝓪𝓬𝓴𝓼𝓽𝓸𝓻𝔂

She was born in Crichton-Vulcan shipyard in 1. August 1930 two months after her older sister. As shipgirls don't age she looked like an adult when she was born and started her training and education really soon. Vesihiisi graduated and entered service in the Finnish navy on 2. December 1931. She has served in two wars, in the Winter War (1939-1940) and the Continuation War (1941-1944). She retired from the navy in 1946 with her sisters. After her retirement she gained interest in Finnish movies and TV. During her retirement she got bored and she wanted to help other people and use her knowledge to teach them about Naval Tactics and Navigation at sea.
