  • Weaknesses: She weighs a lot. She uses a lot more energy compared to a normal person and has to eat accordingly. Recharge time is dependant on the time she was underwater. She emits a quiet rumbling noise (like a quiet diesel engine) at all times except when she sleeps. The more she stresses herself physcally, the louder and faster the noise.
  • Drawbacks: Her abilities work only in water and with her rigging.

She was born in Crichton-Vulcan shipyard in 1. August 1930 two months after her older sister. As shipgirls don't age she looked like an adult when she was born and started her training and education really soon. Vesihiisi graduated and entered service in the Finnish navy on 2. December 1931. She has served in two wars, in the Winter War (1939-1940) and the Continuation War (1941-1944). She retired from the navy in 1946 with her sisters. After her retirement she gained interest in Finnish movies and TV. During her retirement she got bored and she wanted to help other people and use her knowledge to teach them about Naval Tactics and Navigation at sea.
