Background

  • includes references to some unpleasant things some people may not be comfortable with
  • (backstory can be opened by pressing the header)

Aamu was born in eastern Finland on 20th of february to her parents Marjo and Urho Kokko. At a young age she learned from her parents that she wasn't completely normal. She had been checked when she was born and the doctors found out that her respiratory system wasn't normal. Only half of her lungs could process oxygen to the body, but it was almost twice as efficient as a normal human's and the other half processed methane and hydrogen into propane. She started her school at the age of 7 like every other kid in her town. She lived a mostly normal childhood although she did sell propane with her father when she was on summer break from school. One day when she was coming home from school she was kidnapped by some Russian gangsters who had learnt about her ability. The gangsters tortured her to force her to help them. The gangsters had cut her right leg off when the Finnish Police Forces found her and arrested the gangsters, saving her. She was taken to a hospital afterwards, but they couldn't do anything to her leg so they finished the amputation. She was inspired by the police to train herself to become stronger to help others who can't help themselves. She is also a trained car mechanic.
