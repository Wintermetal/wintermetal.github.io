Description

The Company is divided into multiple different subsidies which all produce their own products in a certain field. Some of the subsidies were originally made to serve the needs of the company, but have later grown to offer their services to other companies and individuals as well. Lython Inc. produces a lot of products including, but not limited to: ships, droids and software. The company also offers transport services.

Logo of Lython Heavy Industries

History

Lython Incorporated was founded in 89 BBY by a man named Ledo Lython. He had an interest in spaceship design, but as the company was very small at the time they started off by producing droids under licence for other companies. The B-1 battledroid contracts for the CIS were extremely lucrative for the company and allowed the company to expand to ship building. Shortly before the Clone wars the company got their shipyard ready and they managed to get contracts to manufacture Venator-class star destroyers and other ships for the Republic. The company split their droid production and ship production into separate subsidiaries called Lython Robotics and Lython Heavy Industries. Lython Robotics has received large contracts from the Empire, but they have also sold droids for the Rebel alliance secretly. Although they had always transported their own materials, but finally in 10 BBY they started to offer these services to other companies and individuals under the name Lython Transport.
