Backstory

Matilda was born on 29th of February in 1996. She was born in Silverlock medical centre in Deptford. She spent her childhood living in Deptford. She started her education at Canary Wharf College and continued in George Green's School. She lived her entire childhood in Deptford as her father worked at the Deptford dockyard. After she was finished with school she got some work at the dockyard where she suffered an accident in which she lost the lower portion of her left leg. She chose a wooden peg leg instead of a proper prosthetic as she preferred the look and the roughness of wood.
