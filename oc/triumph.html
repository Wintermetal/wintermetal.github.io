Backstory

As shipgirls don't age she looked like an adult when she was born and started her training and education at a young age. She served in the Royal Navy from 1625 to 1668. She joined the Royal Navy during the Thirty years' war and completed her maiden voyage during a conquest in Cadiz. After the war she sailed with the Royal Navy all across the world patrolling the British colonies. Not long after the Thirty years' war another war broke out with the Dutch known as the First Anglo-Dutch War (1652-1654) She took part in multiple battles in the English channel and the North Sea. She didn't have long to celebrate the victory with her comrades as soon the Anglo-Spanish War (1654-1660) broke out in 1654. She fought in the Battle of Santa Cruz and achieved a crushing victory against the Spanish. She took part in the blockade of Cadiz. After hostilities seized in Europe she was sent to the Caribbean to fight Spanish privateers and pirates who were attacking English ships. After retiring from the Royal Navy, she sailed the seas as a merchant and escorting other merchants and protected them from pirates and privateers. In 1678 she retired from privateer work as she was becoming obsolete and couldn't compete with newer ships. She built herself a house to live alone in the forest in peace. She lived in peace until a school was built next to her house. Triumph was bored as she had very little to do so she decided to begin working as a teacher in the school.
