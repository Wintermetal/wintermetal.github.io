Backstory

Cathrine was born into a loving and hardworking family. Her father worked as a steam engine mechanic and to her mother's disapproval she pursued her father's footsteps. But she didn't want to live her whole life in the same place so when she managed to get a steam lorry she built a home on its bed and left to become a travelling mechanic.
