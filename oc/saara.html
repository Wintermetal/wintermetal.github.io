    • Background

    Saara was born in Turku in 1994 but promptly moved to Jyväskylä in 1995 with her family and then again to a suburb called Annala which was a part of Tampere. Saara went to Annala primary school and in 2007 she switched schools for secondary school and went to Kaukajärvi school. In 2010 Saara went to Police University college located in a part of Tampere called hervanta. Saara was the only child in her family and her parents wanted her to become a doctor and they pressured Saara to study hard and she was top of her class in primary- secondary and in the Police University College. After finishing her studies Saara volunteered for the Finnish army and was accepted into hattula tank brigade only 71,9km away from Tampere. She spent a year in the army and after it she applied for Karhu and successfully passed the tests. Her performance as a Karhu operative raised Six's interest in her and she was invited into team Rainbow.
