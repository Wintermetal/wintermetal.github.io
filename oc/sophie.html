Backstory

Sophie has shown an interest in weapons and weapon design from an early age. One time when she was developing a new rifle on her own the police found out about it and charged her with possession of an illegal firearm as she didn't have a licence for a gun. Agatha Turner read about the arrest in the newspaper and was interested by the gun so she paid the parole to get Sophie out of prison so she could design weapons for Agatha's company Enfield Incorporated. Over time they became good friends and when Agatha left to explore the world Sophie went with her.
