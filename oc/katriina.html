Background

Katriina's mother was a relatively unknown witch who moved to Finland to go into hiding after she got herself into some trouble. She married a Finnish man from a small town and took his surname when they were married. Katriina's father who was a muggle who worked as a car mechanic. She started studying at a muggle school at the age of 7 like all other children in Finland. Because of her childhood and upbringing she is very interested in muggle technology. Her mother taught Katriina all the most important spells as she wanted Katriina to be able to enter the wizarding society. Katriina's mother even taught her to become an animagus, but she doesn't use the power as she doesn't see any benefit in it. In 1985 she bought herself a Saab 900 turbo and enchanted it to make it able to fly as she didn't know how to ride a broom. Later in life she decided that she wanted to become a teacher at the prestigious Hogwarts. She moved to England to apply for the job to teach muggle-studies as she had been living among muggles for a long time.
