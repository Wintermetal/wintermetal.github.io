Backstory

She was born to Mandalorian parents in 23 BBY shortly before the start of the Clone wars. Her mother died in the bombing of Kalevala in 21 BBY. She grew up on Kalevala in relative piece while she was trained by her father. When she turned 18 her father gave her her mother's armour which was a family heirloom. She wanted some adventure and thus she decided to become a bounty hunter as it would allow her to use her training.
