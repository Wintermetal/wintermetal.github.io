Background

LRAC-L517 is a Legion-class titan manufactured by Lython Heavy Industries under license from Hammond Robotics. Lython Heavy Industries manufactured the Legions for the Lython Royal Armoured Corps and around 600 were manufactured. LRAC-L517 entered service with the Lython Royal Armoured Corps in 1.1.1956 (counting by earth years) and was used in mainline combat until his pilot decided to defect the army and took LRAC-L517 as he fled. He escaped in a ship designed to deploy the titans from orbit. When the ship was on Earth's orbit, flying past, it was hit and LRAC-L517 fell out of the ship and down to Earth's surface. Lython Royal Armoured Corps tried to recover LRAC-L517, but failed as the defector had taken the tracker off and reprogrammed LRAC-L517 with new directives. After dropping to earth LRAC-L517 roamed around until he found a lair of people who have similar goal as he does.
