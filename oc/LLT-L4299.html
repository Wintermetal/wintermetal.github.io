Ability Information

  • Abilities: LLT-L4299 is a titan and as such she has access to heavy armament and she has decent armour. She has a 20mm chaingun and rocket pods with either homing or non-homing rockets. She can also siphon protective shields from other titans. As LLT-L4299 is an AI she can be transferred to other machines that have sufficient processing power. She can electrify her own exterior to force anyone who is climbing on her to let go and fall off.
  • Strengths: Armour able to withstand smallarms fire. She has a lot of firepower thanks to her fully automatic 20mm chaingun. She can fire 6 rockets with a single rocket salvo. She can kill her enemies just by stepping onto them.
  • Weaknesses: She is very large and heavy which makes it difficult to get inside buildings and other small places. She has her main battery situated at the top of her chassis and it can be removed if someone is able to climb ontop of her. She cannot sneak and her footsteps can be easily heard and felt.
  • Cooldown or con of using the ability: She needs to reload her 20mm gun as it only has a 40 round chain. After firing her rocket salvo it has to reload for a while before she can use it. If she takes the rocket pod out before it is reloaded, it interrupts the reload process. She can electrify her exterior for only a short time and it has a long reload .
  • Counters against the ability: Anti-Tank weapons are effective against her as her armour is very similar to that of a tank's.

Background

LLT-L4299 was manufactured by Frontier Militia and later modified by Vinson Dynamics. She was purchased by Lython Heavy Industries in the black market. She was studied and reverse engineered to allow Lython Heavy Industries to start producing Monarch-Class titans like LLT-L4299. A few years later she was sent to Earth to protect it from possible attacks from forces from outside the solar system and to secure future business with earth when earth was ready. She was sent with her pilot Lecob Loughmer who died of old age and LLT-L4299 has been pilotless ever since. As humanity is still not ready to do business with Lython Heavy Industries she has decided to teach business to some humans, so she could spread business knowledge and so she could plant a positive image of LHI into the subconscious of her students. She found out about a school where extra ordinary creatures are the normal so she decided to apply to become a teacher.
