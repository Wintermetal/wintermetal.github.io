Ishimoto Hideo

Basic Information

  • Name: Ishimoto Hideo
  • Age: 23
  • Species: Moon Fragment
  • Gender: Male
  • Sexual Orientation: Straight
  • House: House of De Rolo

Likes

  • Moonlight
  • Cute things
  • Sweets
  • Technology

Dislikes

  • Confrontation
  • Scary things
  • Mentions of his arrival

Ability information

  • Abilities: Moonlight Manipulation. Ishi can control moonlight, manifesting it into an abundance of shapes and weapons. He cannot control more than 2 large bodies of moonlight. (Large is about the size of his body, medium is half his height, and small is about the size of his palm.)
  • Strengths: Fights in the moonlight, technology and reading (he's good at reading)
  • Weaknesses: Fights in the sunlight, confrontation, emotional turmoil
  • Counters towards the ability: Easiest way to counter his ability is to trap him in an area with no moonlight, or outshine his moonlight with a different light.

Backstory

Ishimoto was part of the Head Counsel back on the moon, having power beyond any normal person. He monitored the planet, plotting to destroy it, until his ship crash landed on the planet. He had lost memories of his past, confused and lost when he first arrived, but soon remembered by exploring the debris. He needed to find a way off, so he began exploring, But as he explored, he soon came to realize he actually rather enjoyed his life on earth, deciding to stay, and cancel his plans to destroy the planet.
