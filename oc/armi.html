Backstory

Armi was born in Pori which is a part of current day Finland. At an early age she and her family moved to the UK for better employment and education. Later when she was in her 20s she decided to move to the US to become a bounty hunter. In 1898 she found out about her abilities when she died while hunting a bounty and she later respawned nearby. After her first death her other abilities manifested themselves. When the abilities manifested she also stopped aging. Suddenly in 1899 while hunting another bounty she was suddenly taken to somewhere by a portal.
