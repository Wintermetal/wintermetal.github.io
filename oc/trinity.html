Personality

Trinity loves making money and it can be seen in her clothing and level of wealth. Although is quite wealthy she spends it only if necessary and she follows strict rules she has set on herself. She is very greedy, almost on the same level as Scrooge McDuck. She despises thieves and believes they do not deserve to live. Anyone she has spotted stealing from her has not been heard of since. She is often smiling and kind to people who do the same to her and she doesn't want to owe anyone anything so she does her best to repay them.

  • Strengths: kind, if someone helps her she helps them
  • Weaknesses: she is greedy

Ability Information

    Abilities:

    She is basically a ship in human form. She can traverse on water quite quickly.
    Compared to a normal human, she can take a lot more hits (damage?) as she has the same durability as a ship, but she weighs a lot more than a normal human of her size. She needs special equipment to use her power to their full extent.

    Strengths:

    Very tough skin, She can traverse along water relatively fast. She can go for long periods of time without eating or drinking anything, but after that she has to refill her stock. As a side effect of her weight she is quite strong (She can momentarily lift almost 700 tons). Triumph can recover (with the help of a dockyard) from almost any injury. Due to her weight her punches cause immense damage to anything she hits.

    Weaknesses:

    She weighs a lot. She uses a lot more energy compared to a normal person and has to eat accordingly. And everything that comes with a weight of 645 tons. Due to her weight she is very slow when on land. Triumph needs wind to be able to move around on water as she uses sails to move. Triumph can only recover from small injuries by herself, with anything major she has to go to a dockyard for "repairs".

    Counters against the ability:

    Speed and agility.

Background

As shipgirls don't age she looked like an adult when she was born and started her training and education at a young age. Soon she realised that she was quite good at trading goods and she enjoyed making money so she decided to become a merchant and as she traded she started to become very wealthy and respected in her home port. One day after delivering some highly lucrative cargo she was sitting at the port and looking at the sea while wondering what was beyond it. Soon after she decided to see if there was land on the other side and she set sail for the open, unexplored sea in hopes of finding a new land to do business with.
