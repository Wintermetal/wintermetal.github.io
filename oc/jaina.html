Backstory

Jaina was born in Naboo in 34 BBY. She grew up during the clone wars which made her dislike most droids, but she has warmed up to some droids over time. She showed interest in flying starfighters so her father who was a Naboo N-1 starfighter pilot taught her to fly a ship when she was a teenager. When the Republic was replaced by the Empire she wasn't really happy with it, but she didn't do anything about it. Later she started to hear more about the attrocities that the Empire committed and she was talked into joining the Rebellion by her friends. When she joined she was assigned to be a mechanic for the Blue Squadron, but when all the fighters of the squadron were annihilated in 0 ABY she was reassigned to the newish Blade Squadron and at the same time she got a promotion to a B-wing pilot.
