Ability Information

  • Abilities: LRAC-L401 is a titan and as such she has access to heavy armament and she has decent armour. She has a 20mm chaingun and rocket pods with either homing or non-homing rockets. She can also siphon protective shields from other titans. As LRAC-L401 is an AI she can be transferred to other machines that have sufficient processing power. She can electrify her own exterior to force anyone who is climbing on her to let go and fall off. LRAC-L401 has an energy shield that protects her from any hull damage while it is active. Whenever she recharges herself, her shield is also recharged. If she is running low on power the shield is weakened. She can use her thrusters to quickly dodge an attack, but due to her weight they have to recharge for a few seconds.
  • Strengths: Armour able to withstand smallarms fire. She has a lot of firepower thanks to her fully automatic 20mm chaingun. She can fire 6 rockets with a single rocket salvo. She can kill her enemies just by stepping onto them. Her 3 backup batteries are in a somewhat safe position under her left shoulder.
  • Weaknesses: She is very large and heavy which makes it difficult to get inside buildings and other small places. She has her main battery situated at the top of her chassis and it can be removed if someone is able to climb ontop of her. She cannot sneak and her footsteps can be easily heard and felt. Her primary battery is in a vulnerable position ontop of her.
  • Cooldown or con of using the ability: She needs to reload her 20mm gun often as it only has a 40 round chain. After firing her rocket salvo it has to reload for a while before she can use it. If she takes the rocket pod out before it is reloaded, it interrupts the reload process. She can electrify her exterior for only a short time and it has a long recharge.
  • Counters against the ability: Anti-Tank weapons are effective against her as her armour is very similar to that of a tank's.

Backstory

Lython Heavy Industries bought a Monarch-class titan and within a few years they reverse engineered it and built production lines. LRAC-L401 (LLT-L401) was the first one built and sent to serve in the Lython Royal Armoured Corps who sent LRAC-L401 on a scouting mission to a planet in another galaxy.
