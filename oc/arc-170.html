Backstory

The ship was in poor state when it was bought by Lysa, but she repaired the ship to its former glory with her father. They converted the tailgunner's seat to a passenger seat with no controls to allow the transporting of bounties. The pilot and Co-pilot's seat was upgraded to have the controls for the tail gun. The pilot or Co-pilot could have a camera feed from the aft turret for aiming. They repainted the fighter to match Lysa's armour.
