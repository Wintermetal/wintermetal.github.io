Backstory

The Kannus-class of light freighters are manufactured by Lython Heavy Industries and are very common near Lorrd, but quite rare in other parts of the galaxy. The Kannus-class are the standard cargo ship for LHI as they are fast and efficient, but not too expensive to manufacture. The biggest flaw with the ship is that only the cockpit has lifesupport mechanisms to save costs, but as the ships are most of the time piloted by droids this wasn't an issue for LHI. The lack of life support systems led to poor sales outside the companys own uses. The ship is only lightly armed and armoured as it isn't designed for combat so it has the bare minimum to defend itself from fighters.
