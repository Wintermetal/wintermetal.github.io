Backstory

The Kannus-class of light freighters are manufactured by Lython Heavy Industries and are very common near Lipsec, but quite rare in other parts of the galaxy. The Kannus-class are the standard cargo ship for LHI as they are fast and efficient, but not too expensive to manufacture. The ship is only lightly armed and armoured as it isn't designed for combat so it has the bare minimum to defend itself from fighters. The ship was a massive success within Lipsec, but it failed to penetrate the intergalactic market. The ships originally weren't designed with life support systems for other than the cockpit. The ship has multiple choices for under floor compartments. The lowest spec of the ship has a sealed floor so there is no floor access. The medium spec variant has beds in the crouch-height space with the top spec variant adding some storage for some food. The below deck space can be used for smuggling, but this doesn't work near Lipsec as most officials are aware of this below deck space. As the ships weren't popular outside Lipsec Lython Heavy Industries had more ships than there was demand for so they adopted it as their main cargo ship to deliver materials they need, but cannot get from Lipsec. The ships were found to be perfect for LHI's use so they continued to produce them and use them. They have been working on a new and improved version of it, but it has not been completed yet. The ship is available in multiple different specifications, but the highest specification and the lowest specification are the most popular versions of the ships.
