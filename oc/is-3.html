IS-3 "Isukki"

Equipment Information

  • Owner: Leka Seppälä
  • Name: IS-3 "Isukki"
  • The type of material: Rolled homogenous armour, cast homogenous armour (Armour Steel)
  • Functions: 122mm BL-9 main gun, 12.7mm machine gun on the roof and as a co-axial, Can drive at up to 50 km/h
  • Weaknesses/Drawbacks: Very noticable, not very manuverable,
  • Description: Originally made in 1946 served in the Red Army until it was decommissioned and stored on an army depot. In the 80s Leka "borrowed" the tank and took it to the US with her. She modernized the tank so it can be used by a single person.
