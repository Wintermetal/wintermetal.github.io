Backstory

The ship was to be delivered in 19 BBY, but due to Order 66 it never happened so it remained in control of Lython Heavy Industries along with 9 other Venator-class star destroyers which were under construction. The company decided that it would be beneficial to replace their old capital ships with the newer Venator-class star destroyers. The capital ships are used to protect the company's convoys and shipyards, to carry completed ships to dealers and to carry the leaders of the company. The name of the Indefatigable and her sister ships were adopted from the capitalships they replaced.

The dark red colour has been repainted to Medium Nougat
