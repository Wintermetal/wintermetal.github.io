Background

Veikko was born in a small village in Central Finland called Suinula in 1983. As he was in elementary school he saw how some kids were bullying a girl who was very beautiful in his opinion. He saw how she ran away around a corner and the bullies followed her. Soon the bullies came back slightly confused, wondering how she could get away that quickly. He went around the corner, but because of his good sense of smell he knew that she was still there. He wanted to help the girl with the bullies, but didn't know how to so he just left it at that. Later when he was studying to become a teacher he noticed that the girl who he noticed was being bullied in elementary school was in the same school as he was. He got to know the girl who was Pirkko and they fell in love and got married few years later in 2011. When they heard that there was a school for people with abilities like their's, they decided to see if they needed any teachers or nurses.
