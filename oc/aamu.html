Background

  • (includes references to some unpleasant things some people may not be comfortable with)

Aamu was born in eastern Finland on 20th of february to her parents Marjo and Reijo Kokko. She started her school at the age of 6 in her local elementary school and proceeded to secondary school. After she had completed the secondary school she moved to vocational college to study to become a car mechanic. One day when she was coming home from school she was kidnapped my some Russian gangsters who had learnt about her ability whom tortured her to force her to help them. The gangsters had cut her right leg off when the Finnish Police Forces found her and arrested the gangsters saving her. She was taken to a hospital afterwards, but they couldn't do anything to her leg so they finished the amputation. She was inspired by the police to train herself to become stronger to help others who can't help themselves. She applied to Gemora Academy as she had heard that it was a great school for people with abilities.
