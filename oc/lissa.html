Backstory

She was born in 63 BBY to her parents. As she grew up her father taught her to be a great leader as she would some day replace him as the Managing Director of Lython Incorporated. Her childhood was rather uneventful, but when she was 23 she gave birth to a force sensitive child who was named Lindi Larand. During the year after her child was born she became the MD of Lython Incorporated. 2 years later her child was taken away by the jedi order. When her company received an order of Delta-7 starfighters from the Jedi council she made sure her daughter got an improved version that they made a single version of. The special Delta-7 had improved firepower, engines and shields. She wanted to know that her daughter was treated properly so she had spies keep an eye on her daughter.
