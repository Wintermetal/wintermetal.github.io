Background

Kaarina was born and grew up in Turku. She studied to become a teacher as she wanted to help others get better education. One day while she was still studying to become a teacher she was surprised in an alleyway and some people wanted to rob her. When she didn't give her purse they tried to stab her, but her ability activated and turned her skin to titanium and the knife's blade just bent and didn't do any damage to her. She and the robbers were surprised by this and the robbers ran away quickly. It was the first time she had seen her ability work and she didn't know what to think about it. She lived a very uneventful life before coming to work as a teacher at Gemora academy.
