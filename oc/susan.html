Ability Information

  • Abilities: She can materialize any gun manufactured by Webley & Scott. She can materialize ammunition for her guns.
  • Strengths: She can have access to firearms anywhere and everywhere. She has unlimited ammunition*.
  • Weaknesses: Most of her guns are revolvers, pistols or shotguns. Almost all of her firearms are very old designs. She has no fully automatic guns or high-capacity rifles. To make the weapons customized she has to concentrate very hard.
  • Cooldown or con of using the ability: She needs materials the guns are made out of to materialize the gun and the materials have to be within 1 meter of her. She can materialize one gun at a time.
  • Counters against the ability: Bodyarmour or anything that can stop bullets.

*As long as she has materials to materialize them.

Background

Susan was born in Birmingham, England. She is the youngest of the Webleys who are known British firearms manufacturers whom have manufactured weapons for the British Army, the Royal Navy and the RAF. She has had access to Webley manufactured firearms and airguns for her whole life.


She found her ability by accident at the age of 8 when she was at the gun factory making her own Webley Mk VI. When she had all the materials for the revolver in her hand and she was thinking about making the gun, it just materialized in her hand. She experimented with her ability for about a year before she started seriously training her ability. When she was old enough to move to a specialized school, she moved as soon as she could.


Even though she can materialize any Webley manufactured gun, her parents gave her a custom made 900B Series shotgun with her name engraved on it. She fell in love with it so much she always has it with her. She loves her shotgun so much she refuses to use it in combat or let anyone else touch it. She will instead make a similar one she doesn't care about as much.


Susan has a permit to openly carry a firearm in every country she has visited. Due to this she carries her shotgun openly on her shoulder and has gained the nickname "Shotgun girl" in her previous schools.
