Ability Information

  • Abilities: She is basically a ship in human form. She can traverse on water quite quickly and she is very heavily armed: 8x 50.8cm guns, 12x 15cm guns, 16x 10.5cm guns, 28x 3.7cm guns, 40x 2cm guns, 6x 53.3cm torpedoes.
    Compared to a normal human, she can take a lot more hits (damage?) as she has the same durability as a warship, but she weighs about 10 881 times more than a normal human of her size. She needs special equipment to use her power to their full extent.

  • Strengths: Very tough skin, She can traverse along water relatively fast. She can go for long periods of time without eating or drinking anything, but after that she has to refill her stock. As a side effect of her weight she is quite strong (She can momentarily lift up to ~1000 tonnes). Triumph can recover (with the help of a dockyard) from almost any injury.

  • Weaknesses: She weighs a lot. She uses a lot more energy compared to a normal person and has to eat accordingly. And everything that comes with a weight of 131 000 tonnes. She is quite slow on land. Due to this she is quite volatile as she has a lot of ammunition at all times. Triumph can only recover from small injuries by herself, with anything major she has to go to a dockyard for "repairs".

  • Counters against the ability: Land, ashore, ground. Her ability is useless on ground. She needs her equipment to use her abilities.
