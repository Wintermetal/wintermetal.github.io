Backstory

She was born in 497 ABY to her parents, but her mother died while giving birth leaving Lauda with her dad. Even as a little child her father taught her skills that she would need if she were to take his place after he retired. Her father was a loving father, but he wasn't very skilled at showing it and he saw it as very important to teach her the skills she would need. As her father rarely showed Lauda that he loved her she As she grew up her father taught her to be a great leader as she would some day replace him as the Managing Director of Lython Incorporated. Her childhood was rather uneventful, but when she was 23 she became the MD of Lython Incorporated. She had a special affinity towards old ship designs and she scavanged blueprints for old clone war-era ship from the company's vaults of old designs. She brought back many designs, but had asked her engineers to improve them while keeping the design language of the ships and equipment.
