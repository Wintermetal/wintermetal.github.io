Backstory

K-1A2 is a modified, more intelligent KX-series security droid and she was built by Lython Robotics under licence for the Galactic Empire. She is a special version designed to serve in the same role as the Royal guard when the Royal guard aren't available. The droids are also deployed at important locations where more intelligent security droids are needed. Sometimes the droids are deployed to locations where the intelligence of the base model KX-series droid isn't enough. Some Imperial royal security droids took part in the "Night of a Thousand Tears" one of these droids was K-1A2. She survived the night undamaged and afterwards was moved to her previous post.
