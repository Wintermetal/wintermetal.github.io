Ability Information

  • Abilities: He can be "possessed" by the spirit of Kalevala when he holds his smithing hammer.
  • Strengths: When he is "possessed" his strength is doubled and he becomes the best blacksmith on earth, capable of forging items with magical poweres. When possessed he is more resistant to heat. He doesn't become exhausted when he is possessed
  • Weaknesses: When "possessed" he can only speak using the Kalevala measure.
  • Cooldown or con of using the ability: After being possessed he is exhausted like he had ran 2 kilometers. The longer he is possessed the more exhausted he gets. He has trouble communicating with people when he is possessed as he can only talk in Finnish and in a poem like fashion.
  • Counters against the ability: He needs his hammer to be possessed. When he isn't possessed he has no special powers.

Background

Ilmarinen had a normal childhood as he was born without abilities. When he had to pick his career he chose to study to become a machineist as he wanted to work with metal. When he was still a student he made a non-firing prototype of a gun. He couldn't make it fire as he didn't have access to a barrel or anything he could use to make the barrel and he only had mild steel.
